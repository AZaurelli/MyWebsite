The Warm Up

The Warm Up is the second official mixtape from Fayetteville, North Carolina rapper J. Cole. J. Cole and The R.E.C produced the majority of the mixtape with help from Elite, Syience, and E.k.n. The mixtape has been viewed over 3,100,000 times, streamed over 451,000 times, and downloaded over 550,000 times on DatPiff.
